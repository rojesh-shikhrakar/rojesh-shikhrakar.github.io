# Enterprise AI Deployment

## Course Overview

This intensive program is designed to move solopreneurs from "doing everything manually" to orchestrating a high-output business through AI leverage. In a world where a single individual can now operate with the efficiency of a small agency, this course provides the blueprint for building a "Solo Unicorn" but doesn’t guarantee one.

---

## After the program, You'll

- Become better at using AI tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, leveraging most of their features
- Build a Personal/Business Portfolio Website and Deploy them live
- Build Marketing Materials for your business
- Automate certain digital business functions or at least speed up the digital works with AI. 

Note: Note everything can be automated with AI yet. But the world is evolving at a rapid pace.

---

## Module Breakdown

### Week 1: The AI Mindset and Prompt Engineering

Theme: Moving from AI Assistance to AI Collaboration.

- Mindset Shift: Moving from "Shipper" (outcome-only) to "Builder" (system-oriented) to avoid "AI slop" and technical debt.
- Setup & Understand the Ecosystem: Gemini, Claude, ChatGPT, Manus
- The Framework: Mastering the Google Recommended Prompting Technique: Persona, Task, Context, and Format. + other techniques
- Leverage Audit: Identifying high-value tasks vs. low-value administrative tasks.
- Iterative Prompting: Learning to have a "conversation" with AI to refine outputs through multimodal inputs (docs, images, audio).

#### Assignment: 
- Get a Domain Name for your business
- Brainstorm, Perform Market Research with AI to dive deeper and develop your business model (use BMC)
- Build a Personal Portfolio or Landing Page with Claude/Manus, using BMC

### Week 2: Content Creation

Theme: Leverage AI Ecosystem 

- Review, Present your Homework, Improve (SEO, contents, …)
- Deploying your Landing Page to the Web
- Analyze your business
- Image Generation: Generate Graphics for your products 
- Working with Canva
- Working with Google Workspace

#### Assignment:
- Generate Marketing Materials
- Improving & Deploying the Web Page

### Week 3: Content Continued & Social Media Marketing

Theme: Marketing Your Brand

- Review, Present your Homework
- Social Media Marketing: Profile Optimization (FB, Insta, TikTok, LinkedIn, Pinterest, …)
- Audio/Video Generation: Generate Audio Podcasts, Music/Jingle, Video, Explainer video ,...  Gemini, Flow, Vids,...
- Working with Capcut
- Social Media Optimization

#### Assignment:
- Generate Videos for marketing
- Optimize your social media profiles
- Automating Your Posts

### Week 4: Agentic Operations

Theme: Scaling outreach and reducing operational friction through autonomous workflows.

- Review, Present your Homework
- Agentic Workflows: Introduction to multi-step AI agents that can handle research, outreach drafts, and follow-ups.
- Sales Readiness: Using AI to prepare discovery call scripts, objection handling templates, and tailored proposals.
- Operational SOPs: Turning manual tasks into "Standard Operating Procedures" (SOPs) for future automation or delegation.
- Boundary Mapping: Identifying where AI handles "low-level task execution" and where "human judgment" is non-negotiable.
- Service Delivery Optimization: Using AI to improve onboarding, customer success tracking, and feedback collection.
- Responsible AI & Additional Topics

### Assignment:
- Identify and build your own workflow for your business
